# ACGS Design Partner Program

**Date:** 2026-03-19
**Status:** Pre-launch (recruiting)
**Target:** 3-5 partners before Day 1

---

## Program Overview

Design partners receive free access to ACGS Team tier in exchange for feedback, a case study, and logo permission. This program builds the Day Zero pipeline identified in the GTM playbook.

## Terms

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Duration** | 6 months from onboarding |
| **Tier** | Team ($999/month value, $0 cost to partner) |
| **Seats** | Up to 10 users per partner organization |
| **Support** | Direct founder access (Slack channel or email) |
| **Feature input** | Priority consideration for framework and integration requests |

## Partner Obligations

| Obligation | Timeline | Detail |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Onboarding call** | Week 1 | 30 min: deploy ACGS in staging, configure constitution |
| **Monthly feedback** | Monthly | 15 min async (Loom/email) or live call: what works, what doesn't |
| **Case study** | Month 4-5 | Co-authored. Partner approves final version before publication. 500-1000 words |
| **Logo permission** | Month 1 | Written permission to display logo on propriety.ai "Trusted by" section |
| **Quarterly review** | Quarterly | 30 min: roadmap alignment, feature requests, satisfaction check |
| **Reference availability** | Month 3+ | Willingness to take 1-2 reference calls from prospective Enterprise customers |

## Ideal Partner Profile

| Attribute | Ideal | Acceptable |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| **Company size** | 20-100 employees | 10-200 employees |
| **Region** | EU (primary) | US, UK, APAC |
| **Industry** | FinTech, HealthTech, InsurTech, B2B SaaS | Any AI-deploying company |
| **Compliance trigger** | SOC 2 prep, EU AI Act prep, customer contract | General governance interest |
| **Tech stack** | Python, GitLab CI/CD | Any CI/CD, any language with Python bridge |
| **Champion** | Platform engineer or DevOps lead | Any technical decision-maker |
| **AI maturity** | Deploying AI in production | Building AI features |

## Outreach Cadence

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 0 | Personalized email (see messaging/AUDIENCE-NARRATIVES.md) |
| Day 3 | LinkedIn connection request + note (if no email reply) |
| Day 7 | Follow-up email with specific value add (e.g., "I saw your team uses LangChain — here's how ACGS integrates") |
| Day 14 | Final follow-up or close the thread |

**Response rate expectation:** 10-15% from cold outreach with strong personalization. 25-35% from warm intros.

## Onboarding Checklist

For each signed design partner:

- [ ] Signed design partner agreement (email confirmation is sufficient for pre-revenue)
- [ ] Team tier account provisioned on Propriety.ai
- [ ] Founder <> champion Slack channel or email thread established
- [ ] 30-min onboarding call completed
- [ ] ACGS deployed in partner's staging environment
- [ ] First constitution configured (partner's rules or template)
- [ ] First governance validation run successfully
- [ ] Logo permission confirmed in writing

## Exit Criteria

At 6-month mark:

| Outcome | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Partner loves ACGS, wants to continue | Convert to paid Team tier (offer 20% discount for first year as loyalty incentive) |
| Partner is lukewarm | Offer 3-month extension at 50% discount; gather detailed feedback on gaps |
| Partner churns | Exit interview (30 min); document learnings; thank them for participation |

## Legal Notes

- No formal contract required for pre-revenue. Email confirmation of terms is sufficient.
- Logo permission should be explicit ("We permit ACGS / Propriety.ai to display our company logo on their website") — screenshot of email is acceptable.
- Case study requires written partner approval before publication.
- No exclusivity — partners can use competing tools.
- ACGS reserves the right to end the program early with 30 days notice.
