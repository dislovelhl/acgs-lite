# Navigate to Resources Page

## Destination
The ACGS resources page at `/resources`

## Steps

1. From any page on the landing site, click the "RESOURCES" link in the top navigation bar
2. Alternatively, navigate directly to `/resources`
3. Wait for the page to load
4. Verify the heading "Technical Arsenal" is visible
5. Verify two resource categories are displayed: Videos and Presentations

## What You Should See

### Videos Section

Two video resource cards:

1. **Compiling the Law** -- Subtitle: "Engineering HTTPS for AI". Description: "A deep dive into the technical implementation of executable constitutional governance." Has an embedded video player with controls. A "Download High Quality" button below.

2. **Architecting Constraints** -- Subtitle: "Building the MACI Governance System". Description: "Exploring the Montesquieu-Inspired architecture for multi-agent separation of powers." Has an embedded video player with controls. A "Download High Quality" button below.

### Presentations Section

Two presentation resource cards:
